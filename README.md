# Polymatch
• PolyMatch was born from a realization that the sports industry has solved content, scores, and betting, but hasn't solved participation.

• Every match generates millions of conversations, predictions, opinions, and communities, yet that value disappears the moment the game ends.

• We saw an opportunity to build the missing layer: a platform where fan intelligence becomes measurable, reputation becomes valuable, and communities become assets.

• Today's prediction products are features. We are building an ecosystem.

• We are turning predictions into a social graph, leaderboards into reputation systems, and communities into self-sustaining networks.

• Starting with the World Cup gives us access to one of the largest engagement events on the planet, but the vision extends far beyond a single tournament.

• Our goal is to become the infrastructure powering sports participation globally, where millions of fans don't just watch sports, they actively shape the experience.

• We believe the biggest untapped asset in sports isn't data.

• It's fan intelligence.

• PolyMatch exists to unlock it.
